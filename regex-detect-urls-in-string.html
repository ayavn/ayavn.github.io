(Regex) Detect urls in string

Wed 18 May 2022 Wed 18 May 2022
By Hoang Trong Anh
Category: articles
Tags: dart flutter regex regex-url detect-url-in-string

Easy done:

(http|ftp|https):\/\/([\w_-]+(?:(?:\.[\w_-]+)+))([\w.,@?^=%&:\/~+#-]*[\w@?^=%&\/~+#-])
Proudly powered by Pelican, which takes great advantage of Python.
